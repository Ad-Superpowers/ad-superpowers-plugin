# Monthly Executive Summary

Generate an executive-level monthly summary for the previous month.

## Instructions

### 1. Compile Monthly Performance
Aggregate data across all platforms for the full month:
- Total spend and revenue
- Conversions and key actions
- Primary KPIs (ROAS, CPA, etc.)
- Month-over-month trends

- Lead with business impact, not metrics
- Use clear visualizations (tables)
- Provide context for all numbers
- End with clear next steps
