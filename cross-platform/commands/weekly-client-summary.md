# Weekly Client Summary Generator

Generate a professional client-ready summary for the past week.

## Client Context
- Client Type: ecommerce
- Tone: professional

## Instructions

### 1. Gather Performance Data
Pull all relevant metrics for last 7 days across connected platforms.

### 2. Customize for Client Type

**E-commerce Focus:**
- Primary metrics: Revenue, ROAS, Orders, AOV
- Secondary: CPC, CTR, Conversion Rate

**B2B Lead Gen Focus:**
- Primary metrics: Leads, CPL, MQL Rate (if available)
- Secondary: CTR, Reach, Engagement

**Brand Awareness Focus:**
- Primary metrics: Reach, Impressions, Frequency, CPM
- Secondary: Engagement Rate, Video Views

### 3. Output Format

```
================================================================================
                    WEEKLY PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
                    [Client Name] | Week of [Date]
================================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
[2-3 sentences summarizing overall performance, key wins, and focus areas.
Write in professional tone. Be specific with numbers.]

KEY METRICS
-----------
| Metric              | This Week    | Last Week    | Change      |
|---------------------|--------------|--------------|-------------|
| [Primary KPI 1]     | [Value]      | [Value]      | [+/-XX%]    |
| [Primary KPI 2]     | [Value]      | [Value]      | [+/-XX%]    |
| [Primary KPI 3]     | [Value]      | [Value]      | [+/-XX%]    |
| Total Spend         | €X,XXX       | €X,XXX       | [+/-XX%]    |

HIGHLIGHTS & ACHIEVEMENTS
-------------------------
* [Specific win #1 with numbers]
* [Specific win #2 with numbers]
* [Specific win #3 with numbers]

AREAS OF FOCUS
--------------
* [Area needing attention #1 - with context, not alarming]
* [Area needing attention #2 - with context, not alarming]

RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS
----------------------
1. [Actionable recommendation #1]
2. [Actionable recommendation #2]
3. [Actionable recommendation #3]

LOOKING AHEAD
-------------
[Brief note on planned activities, tests, or optimizations for next week]
```

### 4. Tone Guidelines

**Professional tone:**
- Use formal language, third-person perspective
- Focus on data and business impact
- Example: "Campaign performance exceeded targets by 15%."

**Casual tone:**
- Use conversational language, first-person
- Highlight wins enthusiastically, soften concerns
- Example: "Great week! We crushed it with a 15% ROAS boost."
