## Quick Decision Guide

```
WHICH TYPE OF DISPLAY CAMPAIGN DO YOU NEED?
│
├─► Goal: Remarketing / Retargeting
│   └─► STANDARD DISPLAY - REMARKETING
│       ├── Website visitor audiences
│       ├── Cart abandoners, product viewers
│       ├── Bid strategy: tCPA or Maximize Conversions
│       └── Responsive Display Ads
│
├─► Goal: Brand Awareness
│   └─► STANDARD DISPLAY - PROSPECTING
│       ├── Affinity and Custom Audiences
│       ├── Topic/Placement targeting
│       ├── Bid strategy: vCPM (viewable impressions)
│       └── Uploaded image ads + Responsive
│
├─► Goal: Conversions / Lead Gen
│   └─► SMART DISPLAY (or PMax)
│       ├── Automated targeting
│       ├── Google AI optimization
│       ├── Bid strategy: tCPA
│       └── Consider PMax as alternative
│
├─► Goal: App Installs
│   └─► APP CAMPAIGNS (not Display)
│       └── Dedicated app install campaigns
│
└─► Goal: Product Sales (E-commerce)
    └─► PERFORMANCE MAX with feeds
        └── Display as part of PMax
```
