## Competitive Response Strategy

### Response Framework

```
COMPETITIVE RESPONSE DECISION TREE
═══════════════════════════════════

COMPETITOR BECOMES MORE AGGRESSIVE
│
├─► STEP 1: ASSESS IMPACT
│   ├── Your IS dropped >10%? → Significant impact
│   ├── CPA/ROAS deteriorated? → Direct financial impact
│   └── Conversions dropped? → Business impact
│
├─► STEP 2: EVALUATE PROFITABILITY
│   ├── Can we match and remain profitable?
│   │   ├── YES → Consider bid increase
│   │   └── NO → Go to differentiation
│   │
│   └── Calculate break-even CPA/ROAS:
│       └── If competitor wins = above your break-even?
│           └── Let them "win" on unprofitable auctions
│
├─► STEP 3: CHOOSE STRATEGY
│   │
│   ├─► OPTION A: COMPETE DIRECTLY
│   │   ├── Increase bids/targets
│   │   ├── Improve Quality Score
│   │   ├── Expand budget
│   │   └── Best when: You can compete profitably
│   │
│   ├─► OPTION B: DIFFERENTIATE
│   │   ├── Focus on other keywords
│   │   ├── Target other audiences
│   │   ├── Different USPs in ads
│   │   └── Best when: Direct competition too expensive
│   │
│   ├─► OPTION C: NICHE DOWN
│   │   ├── Very specific long-tail
│   │   ├── Geographic niches
│   │   ├── Audience niches
│   │   └── Best when: Large player you can't match
│   │
│   └─► OPTION D: WAIT & MONITOR
│       ├── Track competitor over time
│       ├── Often unsustainable strategies
│       ├── Wait for normalization
│       └── Best when: Competitor seems irrational
│
└─► STEP 4: IMPLEMENT & MONITOR
    ├── Implement chosen strategy
    ├── Track results weekly
    ├── Adjust based on response
    └── Document learnings
```

### Tactical Responses

```
TACTICAL COMPETITIVE RESPONSES
═══════════════════════════════

TACTIC 1: QUALITY SCORE IMPROVEMENT
────────────────────────────────────
Impact: Lower CPCs, better positions without higher bids

Actions:
□ Improve ad relevance (keyword in ad)
□ Improve expected CTR (better ads, extensions)
□ Improve landing page experience
□ Tight themed ad groups

ROI: High (sustainable advantage)

TACTIC 2: BID STRATEGY OPTIMIZATION
────────────────────────────────────
Impact: Smarter bidding, not necessarily more

Actions:
□ Target Impression Share for visibility
□ Portfolio strategies for efficiency
□ Maximize Conversion Value for revenue focus
□ Dayparting around competitor weak moments

ROI: Medium (requires monitoring)

TACTIC 3: COVERAGE EXPANSION
─────────────────────────────
Impact: Win where competitor is not present

Actions:
□ Long-tail keyword expansion
□ Geographic expansion
□ Device-specific campaigns
□ Add audience targeting

ROI: Medium-High (less competitive = cheaper)

TACTIC 4: AD CREATIVE DIFFERENTIATION
──────────────────────────────────────
Impact: Win clicks with better ads, not higher bids

Actions:
□ Unique value propositions
□ Aggressive offers (if possible)
□ Social proof (reviews, ratings)
□ Full extension usage

ROI: High (sustainable, improves QS too)

TACTIC 5: LANDING PAGE ADVANTAGE
─────────────────────────────────
Impact: Win the conversion, not just the click

Actions:
□ Faster page speed
□ Better mobile experience
□ Clearer value proposition
□ Stronger CTAs

ROI: High (improves QS AND conversion rate)
```
