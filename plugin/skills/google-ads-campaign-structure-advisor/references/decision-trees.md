## Quick Decision Guide

```
WHICH STRUCTURE FITS YOUR CASE?
│
├─► NEW ACCOUNT (Greenfield)
│   └─► MODERN SIMPLIFIED STRUCTURE
│       ├── Fewer campaigns, more data per campaign
│       ├── Broad match + Smart Bidding
│       ├── Optimal for machine learning
│       └── See: "Modern Account Structure"
│
├─► EXISTING ACCOUNT WITH MANY CAMPAIGNS
│   └─► CONSOLIDATION NEEDED?
│       ├── Check: <50 conv/campaign/month? → YES
│       ├── Check: Many overlapping keywords? → YES
│       ├── Check: Fragmented budgets? → YES
│       └── See: "Campaign Consolidation"
│
├─► LEGACY SKAG/VERY GRANULAR
│   └─► MODERNIZE
│       ├── SKAGs are outdated for Smart Bidding
│       ├── Consolidate to STAG or Themed
│       ├── Preserve best performers
│       └── See: "SKAG to Modern Migration"
│
└─► HIGH-VOLUME MATURE ACCOUNT
    └─► HYBRID APPROACH
        ├── Separate campaigns where needed (budgets, targets)
        ├── Consolidate where possible
        ├── Portfolio strategies for aggregation
        └── See: "Enterprise Account Structure"
```
