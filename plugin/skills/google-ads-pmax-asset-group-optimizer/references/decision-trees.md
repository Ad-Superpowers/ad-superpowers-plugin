## Quick Decision Guide

```
HOW MANY ASSET GROUPS DO YOU NEED?
│
├─► E-commerce with product feed
│   ├─► <50 products
│   │   └─► 1-3 ASSET GROUPS
│   │       ├── AG 1: Bestsellers / Top products
│   │       ├── AG 2: Category A
│   │       └── AG 3: Category B
│   │
│   ├─► 50-500 products
│   │   └─► 3-7 ASSET GROUPS
│   │       ├── Per product category
│   │       ├── OR per price segment
│   │       └── OR per margin level
│   │
│   └─► 500+ products
│       └─► 5-10 ASSET GROUPS
│           ├── Top performers separate
│           ├── Category-based
│           └── Sale/Clearance separate
│
├─► Lead Generation
│   ├─► Single service/product
│   │   └─► 1-2 ASSET GROUPS
│   │       ├── AG 1: Main offering
│   │       └── AG 2: Alternative angle (optional)
│   │
│   └─► Multiple services
│       └─► 3-5 ASSET GROUPS
│           ├── Per service type
│           └── Separate audience signals per AG
│
└─► D2C / No feed
    └─► 2-5 ASSET GROUPS
        ├── Per product line
        └── Per customer segment
```

### Audience Signal Troubleshooting

```
AUDIENCE SIGNALS ISSUES & FIXES
═══════════════════════════════

PROBLEM: Signals are being ignored
───────────────────────────────────
Symptoms:
├── Insights show different audiences
├── No delivery to your signals
└── AI finds other audiences

Causes & Solutions:
├── Signal lists too small (<1000)
│   └─► Expand lists, combine segments
├── Signals not relevant for product
│   └─► Review and update signals
├── AI finds better audiences
│   └─► Let AI work, monitor results
└── Optimized targeting too aggressive
    └─► Consider audience-only targeting test

PROBLEM: Too broad targeting
────────────────────────────
Symptoms:
├── High CPM but low relevance
├── Many impressions, few conversions
└── Search Terms irrelevant

Solutions:
├── Add more first-party data
├── More specific custom segments
├── Exclude irrelevant audiences
└── More specific search themes

PROBLEM: Too narrow targeting
─────────────────────────────
Symptoms:
├── Limited delivery
├── High CPMs due to limited competition
└── Learning phase takes long

Solutions:
├── Add broader audiences
├── Remove overly specific signals
├── Increase budget for learning
└── Enable optimized targeting
```
