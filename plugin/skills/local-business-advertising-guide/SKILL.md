# Local Business Advertising Guide

## Purpose

Provide a clear, actionable advertising guide for local businesses with limited budgets. Local businesses have different constraints than national brands or e-commerce: small geographic areas, limited budgets, and a need for phone calls, foot traffic, or form submissions rather than online purchases. This guide focuses on maximum impact per euro spent.

## When to Use This Skill

Invoke when user mentions:
- **Local business:** "I'm running ads for a dentist/plumber/restaurant/salon"
- **Small budget:** "I only have €500/month for ads"
- **Local targeting:** "I want to target people in my city"
- **Google Maps/Local:** "How do I show up in Google Maps ads?"
- **Phone calls:** "I want more phone calls from ads"
- **Foot traffic:** "I want more people to visit my store"
- **Local Services Ads:** "What are LSA ads?"
- **Simple setup:** "I don't have time for complex ad management"

## Part 1: Local Advertising Fundamentals

### What Makes Local Different

| Factor | National/E-Commerce | Local Business |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|
| Geography | Nationwide or global | 10-50 km radius |
| Budget | €5K-500K+/month | €500-2,000/month |
| Conversion goal | Online purchase | Call, visit, form fill |
| Buying cycle | Hours to weeks | Same day to 1 week |
| Competition | Hundreds of advertisers | 5-20 local competitors |
| Measurement | Online conversion tracking | Calls, directions, bookings |
| Time to manage | 10-20 hours/week | 30 min-2 hours/week |

### The Local Business Ad Budget Reality

```
Budget: €500/month
├── Google Ads: €350/month (70%)
│   ├── Search campaigns: €300 (~€10/day)
│   └── Performance Max (local): €50 (Google Maps, Display)
└── Meta Ads: €150/month (30%)
    └── Local awareness + lead gen: €150 (~€5/day)

Budget: €1,000/month
├── Google Ads: €700/month (70%)
│   ├── Search campaigns: €550 (~€18/day)
│   └── Performance Max (local): €150
└── Meta Ads: €300/month (30%)
    ├── Local awareness: €150
    └── Retargeting: €150

Budget: €2,000/month
├── Google Ads: €1,200/month (60%)
│   ├── Search campaigns: €800 (~€27/day)
│   ├── Performance Max (local): €200
│   └── Display remarketing: €200
└── Meta Ads: €800/month (40%)
    ├── Local awareness: €400
    ├── Lead generation: €250
    └── Retargeting: €150
```

Note: Google deprecated standalone "Local campaigns" in 2022. Local inventory ads, Maps promotions, and local objectives are now managed through Performance Max campaigns with store goals enabled.

### Why 70/30 Google/Meta for Most Local Businesses

| Channel | Why It Works for Local |
|---------|-----------------------|
| **Google Search (70%)** | People searching "plumber near me" or "dentist Amsterdam" have immediate intent. They need your service NOW. Highest conversion rate. |
| **Meta/Instagram (30%)** | Builds awareness in your area. People aren't searching for you, but they live nearby and might need you soon. Good for staying top-of-mind. |

**Exceptions where Meta gets more budget:**
- Restaurants, bars, events (visual, impulse-driven)
- Beauty salons, wellness (visual, aspirational)
- New businesses needing awareness (nobody knows you exist yet)
- Businesses with strong visual content (before/after photos)

### Local Services Ads (LSA) — The Best Option for Many Local Businesses

### Search Campaign Setup (Simplified)

**Campaign structure for a local business (keep it simple):**

```
Account
└── Campaign: [Service] — [City]
    ├── Ad Group 1: Core Service
    │   ├── Keywords: "plumber amsterdam", "loodgieter amsterdam"
    │   ├── Keywords: "plumber near me", "loodgieter bij mij in de buurt"
    │   └── 2-3 Responsive Search Ads
    ├── Ad Group 2: Specific Service
    │   ├── Keywords: "emergency plumber amsterdam"
    │   ├── Keywords: "drain cleaning amsterdam"
    │   └── 2-3 Responsive Search Ads
    └── Ad Group 3: Brand Terms
        ├── Keywords: "[your business name]"
        └── 1-2 Responsive Search Ads
```

**Why simple works:** With €10-30/day budget, you don't need 20 ad groups. 2-3 focused ad groups with tight keywords will outperform a complex setup that spreads budget too thin.

### Local Keyword Strategy

**Keyword formula for local:**
```
[Service] + [City/Area]
[Service] + "near me"
[Service] + [Neighborhood]
[Urgent modifier] + [Service] + [City]
```

**Examples for a plumber in Amsterdam:**

| Keyword | Match Type | Expected CPC | Intent Level |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| plumber amsterdam | Phrase | €2-5 | High |
| loodgieter amsterdam | Phrase | €2-4 | High |
| emergency plumber amsterdam | Phrase | €4-8 | Very high (urgent) |
| plumber near me | Phrase | €3-6 | High |
| drain cleaning amsterdam | Phrase | €2-4 | High |
| [business name] | Exact | €0.50-1 | Highest (brand) |

**Use `google_ads_run_keyword_planner` to research:**
- Local search volume for your service + city combinations
- CPC estimates for budget planning
- Related keyword ideas you may have missed

### Negative Keywords for Local (Essential)

Add these day one to prevent wasted spend:

| Category | Negative Keywords | Why |
|----------|------------------|-----|
| Jobs | jobs, hiring, salary, career, vacature | People looking for employment |
| DIY | how to, tutorial, diy, zelf doen | People who won't hire you |
| Free | free, gratis, cheap, goedkoop | Low-intent bargain seekers |
| Other cities | [cities you don't serve] | Outside your service area |
| Irrelevant services | [services you don't offer] | Wrong business match |

### Location Targeting

**Set up correctly — this is where local businesses waste the most money:**

| Setting | Recommendation | Why |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| Target radius | 15-30 km (depends on service area) | Only show to people you can serve |
| Location option | "Presence: People IN your location" | NOT "People interested in" (includes tourists, people researching from afar) |
| Bid adjustment | +20% for your immediate area (5 km) | Higher conversion rate for nearby customers |
| Exclusions | Neighboring cities you don't serve | Prevents waste |

**Critical setting:** In Google Ads targeting, change from "Presence or interest" (default) to "Presence: People in or regularly in your targeted locations." The default wastes 20-40% of local budgets.

### Ad Extensions (Use ALL of These)

| Extension | What It Adds | Impact |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Location extension** | Your address + map pin | +10-15% CTR |
| **Call extension** | Click-to-call phone number | +5-10% CTR |
| **Sitelinks** | Links to key pages (Services, About, Reviews, Contact) | +10-20% CTR |
| **Callout** | Short selling points ("24/7 Service", "Free Quote") | +5-8% CTR |
| **Structured snippet** | Service types, areas served | +3-5% CTR |
| **Business name** | Your business name in the ad | Builds recognition |

**Combined effect:** Using all extensions can improve CTR by 20-30% with no additional cost.

## Part 3: Meta Ads for Local Businesses

### Campaign Types for Local

| Objective | Use Case | What You Pay For |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Awareness (Reach)** | Brand building in your area | Per 1,000 impressions |
| **Traffic** | Drive to website or booking page | Per click |
| **Leads (Lead Form)** | Collect contact info without leaving Facebook | Per lead |
| **Messages** | Start Messenger/WhatsApp conversations | Per conversation |
| **Store traffic** | Drive foot traffic to physical location | Per store visit (estimated) |

### Recommended Setup for Local Business

```
Campaign 1: Local Awareness (60% of Meta budget)
├── Objective: Awareness or Traffic
├── Targeting: 15-30 km radius, age 25-65, interests related to your service
├── Creative: Before/after photos, customer testimonials, team photos
└── Budget: €3-5/day

Campaign 2: Lead Generation (40% of Meta budget)
├── Objective: Leads (Lead Form) or Messages
├── Targeting: Same geo, narrower interests or website visitors
├── Creative: Special offer, free consultation, seasonal promotion
└── Budget: €2-3/day
```

### Geo-Targeting on Meta

| Method | When to Use | Setup |
|--------|------------|-------|
| **Radius targeting** | Most local businesses | Drop pin on your location, set 10-25 km radius |
| **Zip code / postal code** | Specific neighborhoods | Enter relevant postal codes |
| **City targeting** | City-wide service | Select city name |
| **"Recently in this location"** | Tourists, visitors | Useful for restaurants, attractions |

### Lead Form Ads (Best for Local)

**Why lead forms work for local businesses:**
- User doesn't leave Facebook/Instagram
- Auto-fills name, email, phone from profile
- 2-5x lower cost per lead than landing page conversion
- Works on low budgets (€5/day can generate 1-3 leads)

**Lead form setup tips:**
1. Keep it to 3-4 questions maximum (name, phone, email, service needed)
2. Add a qualifying question ("What service do you need?" dropdown)
3. Enable instant form (pre-fills data, lower friction)
4. Set up CRM integration or email notification (leads expire after 90 days!)
5. Respond within 1 hour for best conversion to customer

**Expected cost per lead by business type (Meta):**

| Business Type | Cost per Lead | Lead-to-Customer Rate |
|--------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Home services | €5-15 | 20-35% |
| Beauty/wellness | €3-10 | 25-40% |
| Restaurant/bar | €2-5 | 30-50% |
| Professional services | €10-30 | 15-25% |
| Fitness/gym | €4-12 | 20-35% |
| Real estate | €8-25 | 10-20% |
| Automotive | €8-20 | 15-25% |

### Creative That Works for Local

| Creative Type | Format | Works Best For |
|--------------|--------|---------------|
| **Before/after photos** | Image carousel | Home services, beauty, landscaping |
| **Customer testimonials** | Video (30-60s) or image with quote | All local businesses |
| **Team/owner photos** | Image or video | Builds trust, humanizes business |
| **Special offer** | Image with clear offer text | Seasonal promotions, new customer specials |
| **Behind-the-scenes** | Short video (15-30s) | Restaurants, artisans, manufacturers |
| **Google review screenshots** | Image | Any business with 4+ star reviews |

**Local creative rules:**
- Show real photos of your actual business, team, and work (not stock photos)
- Include your city/area name in the ad copy
- Lead with your strongest review or testimonial
- Include a clear next step (Call now, Book online, Get a free quote)

## Part 4: Google Business Profile (Free and Essential)

### Why GBP Matters More Than Ads for Some Local Businesses

```
Before spending €1 on ads, optimize your Google Business Profile.

Why:
- Appears in Google Maps and local pack (3-pack) FOR FREE
- 46% of all Google searches have local intent
- GBP listings get 5x more views than your website
- Reviews on GBP directly influence ad quality and trust
- A well-optimized GBP can generate 50-200 calls/month for free
```

### GBP Optimization Checklist

| # | Item | Impact | Time |
|---|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Claim and verify your business | Critical | 5 min + 1-2 weeks verification |
| 2 | Complete ALL business information fields | High | 20 min |
| 3 | Choose correct primary + secondary categories | High | 5 min |
| 4 | Add 20+ high-quality photos | High | 30 min |
| 5 | Write a keyword-rich business description | Medium | 15 min |
| 6 | Add all services with descriptions | Medium | 15 min |
| 7 | Set accurate hours (including holiday hours) | Medium | 5 min |
| 8 | Enable messaging | Medium | 2 min |
| 9 | Add products/menu (if applicable) | Medium | 20 min |
| 10 | Post weekly updates (offers, news, events) | Medium | 10 min/week |
| 11 | Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours | High | 5 min per review |
| 12 | Ask happy customers for reviews | High | Ongoing |

### Review Strategy

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Star rating | 4.5+ | Below 4.0, you lose significant clicks |
| Review count | 50+ (100+ ideal) | Volume signals popularity and trust |
| Review recency | New reviews every week | Stale reviews signal inactive business |
| Response rate | 100% | Responding shows you care (positive AND negative) |
| Review keywords | Mention services + city | Helps ranking for those terms |

**How to get more reviews:**
1. Ask at the point of happiness (right after successful service)
2. Send a follow-up text/email with direct Google review link
3. Make it easy: "Leave us a Google review" with QR code at register/reception
4. Respond to every review with a personalized thank you
5. Never buy fake reviews (Google detects and penalizes)

## Part 5: Conversion Tracking for Local

### What Counts as a Conversion

| Conversion Type | Value | How to Track |
|----------------|-------|-------------|
| **Phone calls** | Highest | Call extensions (Google), call tracking number |
| **Form submissions** | High | Thank-you page conversion event |
| **Direction requests** | Medium | Google Maps directions click tracking |
| **Booking/appointment** | Highest | Booking confirmation page event |
| **WhatsApp/Messenger clicks** | Medium | Click tracking event |
| **Store visits** | Medium | Google estimated store visits (larger businesses only) |

### Simple Tracking Setup (Minimum Viable)

**If you do nothing else, set up these 3 things:**

1. **Google Ads call tracking:** Enable call extensions with Google forwarding number. Google tracks calls of 60+ seconds as conversions automatically.

2. **Form submission tracking:** Add a Google Ads conversion tag on your "Thank You" page (or use GTM to track form submissions).

3. **GA4 with Google Ads linking:** Link GA4 to Google Ads to see which keywords drive form submissions.

**Use `google_ads_run_gaql` to check conversion setup:**
```sql
SELECT
  conversion_action.name,
  conversion_action.type,
  conversion_action.status,
  metrics.conversions,
  metrics.all_conversions
FROM conversion_action
WHERE conversion_action.status = 'ENABLED'
```

## Part 6: Budget Management

### Monthly Budget Planning

```
Monthly budget: €1,000

Fixed costs:
  Tool/software: €0 (Google Ads + Meta are free to use)
  Domain/hosting: N/A (already paying)

Variable ad spend:
  Google Ads: €700 (€23/day)
  Meta Ads: €300 (€10/day)

Expected results (conservative estimates):
  Google clicks: 200-400/month (€1.75-3.50 CPC)
  Google conversions: 20-40 leads (10% conversion rate)
  Meta reach: 15,000-30,000 people/month
  Meta leads: 15-30 leads (from lead forms)

  Total estimated leads: 35-70/month
  Cost per lead: €14-29
```

### When to Increase Budget

| Signal | Action | How Much |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Impression share < 50% (Google Search) | You're losing half of searches to competitors | Increase 20-30% |
| All leads converting to customers | Demand exceeds capacity? Scale when ready | Increase 10-20% |
| Seasonal peak approaching | Ramp up 2-4 weeks before busy season | Increase 30-50% |
| New competitor entering market | Defend your position | Increase 15-25% |
| Budget maxes out by 2 PM daily | Missing afternoon/evening searches | Increase until budget lasts all day |

### When NOT to Increase Budget

| Signal | Issue | Fix First |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Leads not converting to customers | Sales/follow-up problem, not ad problem | Fix response time, follow-up process |
| High bounce rate on landing page | Website problem, not ad problem | Improve landing page |
| CPC rising but conversions flat | Targeting or relevance issue | Optimize keywords, improve ad relevance |
| Competitors undercutting on price | Pricing problem | Adjust positioning, highlight value |

## Part 7: DIY vs Hiring an Agency

### Decision Framework

```
DIY if:
  ✓ Budget under €500/month (agency fees eat the budget)
  ✓ Simple service area (1 city, 1-2 services)
  ✓ You have 2-4 hours/month to manage
  ✓ You're willing to learn the basics
  ✓ You can use tools like AdSuperpowers MCP for guidance

Hire an agency if:
  ✓ Budget over €2,000/month (agency fee becomes worthwhile)
  ✓ Multiple locations or service areas
  ✓ Complex service offering (many campaigns needed)
  ✓ No time or interest in learning ad platforms
  ✓ You need multi-platform strategy (Google + Meta + more)

Consider a freelancer if:
  ✓ Budget €500-2,000/month
  ✓ Need help with setup, then can maintain
  ✓ Want monthly check-ins without full management fees
```

### Typical Agency Fee Structures for Local

| Model | Cost | What You Get |
|-------|------|-------------|
| % of ad spend | 15-25% of monthly spend | Full management, reporting |
| Flat monthly fee | €300-800/month | Fixed scope management |
| Setup + hourly | €500-1,500 setup + €50-100/hr | Initial build + as-needed |
| Performance-based | Cost per lead markup | Pay for results only |

## Part 8: Common Local Advertising Mistakes

### The 10 Most Expensive Mistakes

| # | Mistake | Cost Impact | Fix |
|---|---------|-------------|-----|
| 1 | **Targeting "Presence or interest"** instead of "Presence only" | 20-40% wasted spend | Change Google Ads location setting |
| 2 | **No negative keywords** | 15-30% wasted on irrelevant clicks | Add negatives day one (see list above) |
| 3 | **Too broad an area** | Paying for clicks 50+ km away | Tighten radius to actual service area |
| 4 | **No call tracking** | Can't measure #1 conversion type | Enable Google call forwarding |
| 5 | **Not responding to leads quickly** | 50-80% of leads go cold after 1 hour | Set up mobile notifications, call back within 15 min |
| 6 | **Ignoring Google Business Profile** | Missing free leads from Maps | Optimize fully (see checklist) |
| 7 | **Stock photos in Meta ads** | Low engagement, looks generic | Use real photos of your business |
| 8 | **No reviews strategy** | Competitors with more reviews win | Ask every happy customer |
| 9 | **Running ads to homepage** | Visitors can't find what they searched for | Send to specific service page |
| 10 | **Set and forget** | Performance degrades over time | 30 min/week management (see below) |

### Quick Health Check GAQL

Use `google_ads_run_gaql` for a fast local account health check:

```sql
SELECT
  campaign.name,
  metrics.impressions,
  metrics.clicks,
  metrics.conversions,
  metrics.cost_micros,
  metrics.search_impression_share,
  metrics.average_cpc
FROM campaign
WHERE campaign.status = 'ENABLED'
  AND segments.date DURING LAST_7_DAYS
ORDER BY metrics.cost_micros DESC
```

**Use `meta_get_insights` for Meta performance:**
- Level: campaign
- Fields: impressions, reach, clicks, actions (lead, messaging_conversation_started)
- Date range: last 7 days

## Quick Start: Launch Local Ads in 1 Hour

For the business owner who wants to start TODAY:

```
Minute 0-15: Google Ads Setup
  1. Create Google Ads account
  2. Link to Google Business Profile
  3. Create 1 Search campaign with your core service + city
  4. Add 5-10 keywords (service + city, service + near me)
  5. Add negative keywords (jobs, free, diy, how to)
  6. Set location to your service radius (Presence only!)
  7. Enable call extension with your phone number
  8. Set budget to €10/day
  9. Launch

Minute 15-30: Meta Ads Setup
  1. Create Meta Business page (or use existing)
  2. Create 1 Lead Generation campaign
  3. Target: 15 km radius, age 25-65
  4. Upload 3-5 real photos of your business/work
  5. Write ad: [Your service] in [City]. [Key benefit]. Book now.
  6. Create lead form: Name, Phone, Service Needed
  7. Set budget to €5/day
  8. Launch

Minute 30-45: Google Business Profile
  1. Claim/verify your listing
  2. Add all business info
  3. Upload 10+ photos
  4. Ask 5 recent customers to leave a review today

Minute 45-60: Tracking
  1. Enable Google Ads call tracking
  2. Set up email notifications for Meta leads
  3. Create a simple spreadsheet to track leads

DONE. Revisit in 1 week to check results.
```
