## Quick Decision Guide

```
IS DEMAND GEN RIGHT FOR YOU?
│
├─► Do you have visually strong assets?
│   ├─► No → Consider Search or PMax first
│   └─► Yes ↓
│
├─► What is your primary goal?
│   ├─► Direct response (sales/leads)
│   │   └─► DEMAND GEN + CONVERSION BIDDING
│   │       ├── tCPA or Maximize Conversions
│   │       ├── Product feeds possible
│   │       └── Focus on high-intent audiences
│   │
│   ├─► Brand awareness
│   │   └─► DEMAND GEN + REACH BIDDING
│   │       ├── Maximize Clicks or tCPM
│   │       ├── Broad targeting
│   │       └── Frequency management
│   │
│   └─► Consideration/engagement
│       └─► DEMAND GEN + ENGAGEMENT FOCUS
│           ├── Maximize Clicks
│           ├── Lookalike audiences
│           └── Retargeting of engaged users
│
├─► Which placements to prioritize?
│   ├─► YouTube Home/Watch Next → Video-first creatives
│   ├─► Discover Feed → Image-first creatives
│   ├─► Gmail Promotions → Email-style creatives
│   └─► YouTube Shorts → Vertical video (9:16)
│
└─► Budget available?
    ├─► <€30/day → Start with Search/PMax first
    ├─► €30-100/day → Single Demand Gen campaign
    └─► >€100/day → Segmented Demand Gen strategy
```
