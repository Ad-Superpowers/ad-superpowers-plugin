## Example Interaction

**User:** "I just took over a new Google Ads account. Can you run an audit?"

**Claude Response:**

I'll help you with a systematic account audit. Let's start by gathering basic info and then work through a structured audit.

---

**STEP 1: Basic Information**

Can you tell me:
1. Account type: E-commerce or Lead Gen?
2. Monthly budget: ___
3. Current KPIs (CPA/ROAS targets)?
4. How long has this account been running?
5. Which campaign types are active (Search, PMax, Display)?

---

**STEP 2: Quick Health Check (5 minutes)**

Open Google Ads and check these items:

- **Conversions (last 7 days):** How many?
- **Campaigns with warnings:** Are there "Limited" or "Learning" statuses?
- **Tracking status:** Open Goals -> Conversions - any red warnings?

---

**STEP 3: Deep Dive (after basic info)**

Based on your answers I'll run through the full audit checklist:

1. Tracking & Data Quality
2. Account Structure
3. Keyword Strategy (Search)
4. Ad Copy & Creative
5. Bidding & Budget
6. Audiences
7. Performance Metrics

Share the basic info and I'll start the detailed audit with health score and action plan!
